About Knot Art

Hi, I'm Laura and I'm the macrame artist behind Knot Art. I make beautiful macrame wall hangings from natural materials, which will bring a sense of calm to your home!

You can browse and buy my pieces from my Shop.

I also regularly have a stall at in-person craft markets, which are all listed on the Markets page. At these markets you can get to see and feel the pieces before buying.

If you sign up for an account with us, you can save a market to your profile, that way you won't miss out or forget about the market you want to go to! You'll be able to view your saved markets, as well as any order history, once you have an account set up.

Sign up to my newsletter to get notified about new products, upcoming markets and special sale events!
