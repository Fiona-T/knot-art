About Us

Hi, I'm Laura and I'm the macrame artist behind Knot Art. I make handmade macrame wall hangings from natural materials, which will bring a sense of calm to your home!

If you're a fan of nature inspired interior design, natural decor, or textile art, then look no further! I craft these pieces from natural yarn, using different macrame knots, and driftwood or found wood as hangers. My pieces work well in bedrooms or living rooms for a natural or bohemian inspired interior.

You can browse and buy macrame wall hangings from my Shop here. You'll find a mix of large wall hangings, both natural and colourful hangings, some dream catchers and some mini wall hangings. Each piece is handmade and unique.

I also regularly have a stall at in-person craft markets, which I list on the Markets page. I'm based in Dublin, Ireland so most of these will be located in and around Dublin or other counties in Ireland. Coming to a market gives you the chance to see and feel the wall hangings before buying.

If you sign up for an account with us, you can save the markets you want to go to, and you can also get involved in the conversations with our community by commenting on a market. You'll be able to view your saved markets, as well as any order history, once you have an account set up.

Sign up to my newsletter to get notified about new products, upcoming markets and special sale events!
